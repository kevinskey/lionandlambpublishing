Got questions

Frequently asked

How many copies can I print?+
Your PDF license covers unlimited printing for a single ensemble — one school, one program. You can print as many individual parts as your group needs.
Can I pay by school purchase order?+
Yes. Select "Purchase Order" at checkout and upload your PO document or enter your PO number. We'll invoice your district's billing department and send the download link immediately — no need to wait for payment.
What format are the files?+
All digital scores are delivered as high-resolution PDF files, optimized for both printing and screen display. Most titles also include a separate conductor score PDF.
What if I lose my download?+
Every purchase is stored permanently in your Forte account library. Log in at any time and re-download — no expiry, no extra charge.
Can I share the file with another director?+
Licenses are per ensemble. If another director at a different school needs the same piece, they'll need their own purchase. Sharing files between schools isn't permitted under the license terms.
