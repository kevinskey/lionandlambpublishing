Appalachian Dawn opens with a serene, folk-inspired melody in the woodwinds, evoking the misty stillness of an Appalachian morning before the rest of the ensemble gradually awakens. Hendricks draws on traditional modal harmonies, weaving them through a contemporary concert band texture.

The piece builds through three distinct sections: a pastoral opening, an energetic dance-like middle, and a triumphant return of the opening theme fully scored for the entire band. The finale features a chorale-style coda that brings the music to a warm, resonant close.
